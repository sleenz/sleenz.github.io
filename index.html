Publications

Research papers and academic contributions

Demographic Dynamics and Urban Planning: Analyzing Population Growth Trends in the Four Asian Tigers

Enzo O. Taslim, Febryano H. Detesta, Gandhi P. Perwira, Liora L. Theadora, Yong C. Xuan

The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Department of Computing, 2024

Comprehensive analysis of population growth trends in Hong Kong, Singapore, South Korea, and Taiwan using advanced statistical methods including Monte Carlo simulations, Gompertz modeling, and time series decomposition. Forecasts demographic shifts through 2053 with implications for infrastructure planning, resource allocation, and sustainable urban development in rapidly urbanizing economies.

PDF

The Democratic Immune System: How Democratic Dispositions Shaped Divergent Political Trajectories in Estonia and Hungary

Enzo Orlando Taslim, Craig McMayabi Kandiro, Ashley Queensa, Liora Lyn Theodora

The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Department of Applied Mathematics, 2025

Comparative analysis of democratic resilience in post-Soviet states using European Social Survey data (n=31,813) and V-Dem Democracy Index. Developed a three-pillar framework measuring civic engagement, institutional trust, and political participation to predict democratic backsliding. Successfully forecasted Hungary's authoritarian shift under Orbán while explaining Estonia's sustained democratic stability from 2004-2020.

PDF

Financial Analysis of PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk: Q3 2025 Performance Review

Enzo Orlando Taslim

The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, 2025

Comprehensive financial analysis of Indonesia's leading consumer goods company revealing exceptional margin expansion (+180 bps operating margin to 16.1%) despite revenue stagnation (0.7% growth). Identified material contingent liability of Rp1.38 trillion in tax disputes (41% of equity) and evaluated strategic implications of pending Rp7 trillion ice cream divestiture. Analysis covers profitability metrics, working capital efficiency, and capital allocation strategies.
